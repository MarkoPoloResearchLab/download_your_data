Get a copy of your data from social & search platforms

Facebook

Export posts, photos, messages, and more via Facebook’s Download Your Information tool.

  1. Go to Settings → Your Facebook Information → Download your information.
  2. Choose Date range (All time), Format (HTML or JSON), and Media quality (High recommended).
  3. Select categories or keep all selected, then click Create file.
  4. When notified, return to Available files and click Download (link expires in a few days).

Official help: How do I download a copy of my Facebook information?

Instagram

Request your photos, comments, profile info, and more. Instagram emails a download link.

  1. App: Profile → menu (≡) → Your activity.
  2. Tap Download your information (or Download or transfer information).
  3. Choose Complete copy, Delivery (To your device), Format (HTML or JSON), Quality, and Date range.
  4. Tap Request download, confirm password. Link is time-limited.

Official help: Get a copy of your information

LinkedIn

Export a full archive or specific items (connections, messages, profile, etc.).

  1. Desktop: Me → Settings & Privacy → Data privacy.
  2. Select Get a copy of your data.
  3. Choose Larger data archive (full) or select specific files.
  4. Click Request archive, confirm password. Email arrives when ready (~24h).

Official help: Download your account data

TikTok

Request a ZIP of your account data from the mobile app.

  1. Profile → menu (☰/⋯) → Settings and privacy → Account.
  2. Tap Download your data.
  3. Choose Format (JSON recommended), then Request data.
  4. Return to Download data to fetch the ZIP when ready (time-limited).

Official help: Requesting your data

X (Twitter)

Get an archive including Tweets, media, DMs, and profile info.

  1. Web: More → Settings and privacy → Your account.
  2. Click Download an archive of your data.
  3. Verify identity (password & verification code), then Request data.
  4. When notified, return and click Download archive to save the ZIP.

Official help: How to download your archive

YouTube

Export your YouTube and YouTube Music data via Google Takeout.

  1. Open Google Takeout and sign in.
  2. Click Deselect all, check YouTube and YouTube Music.
  3. Click Next step, set delivery, frequency (Export once), file type (.zip), size.
  4. Click Create export. Download from the email within ~7 days.

Official help: Download your data (Google Takeout)

Google (All Account Data)

Export Gmail, Drive, Photos, Maps, YouTube, and more via Google Takeout.

  1. Open Google Takeout and sign in.
  2. Leave all services checked or Deselect all and pick the ones you need.
  3. Click Next step, set delivery, frequency, file type (.zip), and size.
  4. Click Create export; download from the email within ~7 days. Store securely.

Official help: Download your data (Google Takeout)

Security tip: Data exports can contain sensitive information. Store your ZIPs in a secure, encrypted location.
